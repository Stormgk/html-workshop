Cotton eyed Joe

The "Cotton-Eye Joe" meme, particularly its version with the Roblox man face, has taken the internet by storm, starting with a gibberish-laden cover of the song by Rednex and gaining traction on TikTok in January and February 2024.

The meme features characters singing the song, often paired with the Roblox Male Face and misheard lyrics speech bubbles, leading to its resurgence. This page aims to explore the origins, spread, and impact of the "Cotton-Eye Joe" Roblox meme.

Origin and Spread

•Origin: The meme began with a TikTok cover by @razioff in July 2023, which was later popularized by the Roblox Chicken Nugget account on TikTok.

•Spread: It gained significant attention with TikTokers like @wenonugget and @officialomegahamster, leading to a viral trend in 2024.

Key Figures and Influences

•Roblox Chicken Nugget: A series of videos featuring a chicken nugget with the Roblox man face singing and dancing along to "Cotton Eye Joe."

•@razioff: The original TikToker who posted the gibberish-laden cover, sparking the meme's popularity.
